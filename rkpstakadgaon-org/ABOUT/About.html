ABOUT US

About us

Aadhar Foundations The Cambridge Valley International School is CBSE Affiliated School which has classes from nursery to X standard. In the near future we plan to start a stream for higher classes.
The School is located in a prominent area of Georai, Maharashtra and built over a six acre area. The Cambridge Valley International School have dedicated campus, world class facilities and best educational methodologies. each class contains only 40 students to maintain good quality of education. Our classrooms make optimum use of technology and all are equipped with audio visual equipments.
The aim of the school is to develop the child in an all round manner with focus on the child's physical, intellectual, social, emotional and spiritual well being. The school strives hard to help the children grow with a love for learning. The school helps the children, acquire critical thinking, and moral values needed to become good citizens of tomorrow.
The well planned school campus houses academic blocks, administrative blocks, food court, staff quarters, swimming pool, amphitheatre and outdoor & indoor game facilities all within easy reach.
Our unique nurturing approach will give your child the opportunity to develop into a joyful, confident learner who is prepared for success in school and in life.

Aim

The Aim of The Cambridge Valley International School is to create future leaders using Technical revolution and instilling values.

Lush Green Campus

We have Lush Green Campus Sprawling over more than 6 acres. We have big ground where swimming pool, badminton court, lawn tennis court and tennis court are available. The emphasis of the school's design is on spaces that are cheerful, orderly, visually calm and hygienic all vital environmental components in the imparting of a quality education. Multiple playgrounds fulfill all variety of sporting requirements.

Academic Building

The Academic building has been built taking into consideration all the infrastructural needs & international norms for a school. Every class contains 40 students. Each classroom occupies a large area and is designed such that every student gets the teacher's maximum Individual attention. All the classrooms are well. Ventilated. Each classroom is equipped with a projector and a visualize. Some of the topics are taught with the help of this technology so as to make learning an Interactive.

Sports Complex

To encourage our students to be all-rounder in sports we have badminton court, lawn tennis court basketball court and low depth swimming pool. A variety of indoor and outdoor activities are conducted in the school. The school has appointed a trained Physical Trainer to train the children in various indoor and outdoor games. We encourage students to participate in all the sports events.

Canteen

The Cambridge Valley International School provides a vegetarian diet for its pupils cooked in hygienic kitchen under the direction of a well qualified and experienced catering staff. Menu is kept changing every week which contains all nutritional value and at the same time every student will enjoy the tasty food. It will include breakfast lunch. Food is always checked by headmaster for quality.

CCTV Surveillance

Our school campus is under CCTV Surveillance. We monitor and keep track of activities taking place in school premises. We keep track of attendance of students, teachers and support staff along with ensuring they are all doing their work efficiently. CCTV Surveillance is useful in case of any emergencies. Safety of our students is very important to us so that parents have not to worry about their safety.

Experienced and Qualified Staff

We have experienced & qualified staff from different states of India. They have been very carefully selected out of the best available talents. These teachers are imbued with the spirit of dedication and devotion. They are not only qualified but also have excellent personality and teaching background. Our teachers create friendly environment with every child. Our teachers ignite a natural curiosity and love for learning in our learners and guide them in their quest for knowledge.

Laboratory

We have three laboratories for our students: Math’s Lab, Science Lab and Computer Lab. Dedicated and trained teachers are always at hand to help students with research and project work. Student usage of media and technology in computer lab is monitored and supervised by teachers. Students perform the entire science practical under the supervision of teachers. In math’s lab students learn different easy techniques of doing math’s and intensive practice of hard problems.

Fun while learning

Our approach is based on widely accepted research that indicates that children experience the deepest, most genuine learning when they are having fun. Highly trained teachers foster the cognitive and social development of each child by lovingly guiding the class through play-based activities. We also conduct different activities like story telling fruit day, flower day, field trip and many more.

Transport Facility

Aadhar Foundations provides transport facility for school The Cambridge Valley International School for the safe and comfortable commutation of children from house to school. We have ensured that every bus is managed by well-trained and experienced drivers equipped with cell phone facility. Every Bus is equipped with GPS tracking devices. Every bus also has a female attendant who is there throughout the journey. In addition, our staff is also present on the school buses.
