ACTIVITY

Home work

Homework is considered to be an integral part of the learning process encouraging independent study. Home work is planned for each subject meticulously. To increase the involvement of the parents in the curriculum of their children and to introduce latest technologies into educational system for the welfare of the children the school introduced “Prism’s Mobile Application”. This smart phone application enables the teachers to share home work, activities & important notices with the parents.

Inter School Opportunities

We at The Cambridge Valley International School are driven to enable each student to realize his/her potential to the fullest & achieve their dream. Thus, we encourage & motivate students to participate in inter school competitions.

School and Parents

Parent teacher meetings are held regularly to understand every individual’s learning capabilities and also their social, intellectual and emotional development. Parents are invited to watch their children perform during special assemblies, celebrations and functions, projects displays. There is a regular communication through telecommunication detailed circulars, children’s diaries & message service.

Discipline

The Cambridge Valley International School prides itself on good working atmosphere created by a well-disciplined environment. We intend to focus on positive rather than on negative behavior. We endeavor to instill a sense of self discipline in the children. However, we do enforce appropriate disciplinary measures when necessary.

Overview of the 4 houses

There are 4 houses at S.S.P.S., namely – Jupiter, Mars, Mercury and Venus. These houses are clubbed together for various Inter-House competitions and calendared events. Each House is headed by a Housemaster and a Housemistress who are assisted by a number of Teachers (called as Mentors/Tutors) attached with the Houses. To take care of the pastoral, moral and emotional needs of the children, they are placed under the direct supervision of mentors with a standard ratio of 1:10. The mentors occupy the position as the first line of contract discharging their duties and responsibilities and play a vital role in character building of the children.
