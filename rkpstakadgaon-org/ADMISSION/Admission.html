ADMISSION

Admission Procedure

  1. The Admissions for the new Academic session start in Feb. / March every year.
  2. Admissible age for JR. KG is 4 years.
  3. Admissible age for Grade 1 is 6 +.

Documents Required for Admission:

  1. Students seeking admission for Pre-Primary and Grade I require to submit:
    1. Original Birth Certificate.
    2. Two passport size photographs of the student
    3. Health record / Medical history
  1. Students seeking admission for Grade 2 and above will require to submit following documents:
    1. Original Transfer Certificate.
    2. Attested photo copy of the mark sheet from the previous school.
    3. Two passport size photographs of the student.
    4. Health record / Medical history.
